Your Transformation Starts Here

Muscle Max Fitness isn't just a gym - it's a lifestyle. Founded in 2015 by professional bodybuilder Rajesh Kumar, we've grown to become Pune's premier fitness destination with over 500 members achieving incredible results.

Our 10,000 sqft facility in Bibwewadi near Salve Garden features state-of-the-art equipment, dedicated training zones, and recovery amenities to support every aspect of your fitness journey.
